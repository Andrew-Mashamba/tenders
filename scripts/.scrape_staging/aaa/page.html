TRIPLE 'A' ARCHITECTS
Sustainable architecture

We are a Tanzanian architectural firm based in Arusha, offering tailored design solutions that blend functionality, aesthetics, sustainability, and cost-efficiency. Since 1986, we have specialised in an environmentally conscious design approach rooted in local insight and driven by real-world outcomes.

At Triple 'A' Architects, our team of over 25 professionals—including architects, landscape designers, interior designers, and project managers—works collaboratively from concept to completion. Whether it’s a home, hospitality project, commercial space, or public institution, we deliver innovative, context-driven designs that transform your vision into sustainable, inspiring architecture.
