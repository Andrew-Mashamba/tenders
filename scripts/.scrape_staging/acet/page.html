WELCOME TO ACET

Dear Esteemed Visitors,
It is with great pleasure and pride that I welcome you to the official website of the Association of Consulting Engineers Tanzania (ACET). As the President of ACET, I am honored to extend a warm greeting to all engineers, professionals, stakeholders, and everyone who have chosen to explore our online platform. Here, you will discover a wealth of information about our organization, its mission, values, and the opportunities we offer to both experienced experts and emerging talents in the field of consulting engineering. At ACET, we are committed to promoting excellence, professionalism, and ethical conduct in the practice of consulting engineering across Tanzania and beyond. Through this website, we aim to foster a vibrant community of engineers, facilitate knowledge-sharing, and provide valuable resources to support your professional growth and development. Whether you are seeking networking opportunities, access to industry insights, or looking to engage with like-minded individuals, ACET is your trusted partner on this journey. Join us, and let’s embark on a collaborative venture towards shaping the future of consulting engineering together.

A BIT ABOUT ACET

﻿The Association of Consulting Engineers Tanzania (ACET) is a non-profit organization established in 1985 to promote the practice of consulting engineering in Tanzania with a focus on professional ethics and business integrity. With a membership comprising over 180 local professionals and firms, ACET represents a diverse range of engineering leovegas casino leovegas casino online disciplines, including civil, structural, electrical, mechanical, and environmental engineering. As a member of the International Federation of Consulting Engineers (FIDIC) since 1986, ACET aligns itself with global standards and best practices in the consulting engineering industry.
