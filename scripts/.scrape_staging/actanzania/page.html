CALL FOR PROPOSALS: independent audit of the Council’s 2024 financial report

ACT Tanzania News 28 November 2025

The Agricultural Council of Tanzania (ACT) invites proposals from qualified and reputable audit firms to conduct an independent audit of the Council’s 2024 financial report. The purpose of this audit is to enhance transparency, ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations, and strengthen accountability to our stakeholders.

Interested and eligible audit firms are encouraged to submit their proposals in accordance with the guidelines provided in the attached Call for Proposals.

Application Timeline:
The submission window is open for 7 days (one week) starting from today. Late submissions will not be considered.

We look forward to receiving proposals from capable firms committed to delivering high-quality, professional audit services.

Issued by:
Agricultural Council of Tanzania (ACT)

RICE PROCESSING FACILITY BY ACT-BFC

ACT Tanzania News 20 November 2025

As part of its ongoing commitment to strengthen the rice value chain, ACT, with generous support from the Beyond Farming Collective (BFC),has successfully established a modern rice processing facility in Mkindo Village, Mvomero District,Morogoro Region. The initiative aims toempower local rice farmers through improved processing efficiency, enhanced product quality, and better market returns.
