## Scope of Work

End-to-end support across the AI/ML lifecycle:

- **Use Case Development:** ideation, design, prototyping, scaling (fraud detection, credit scoring, customer experience, financial forecasting, agentic AI, chatbots)
- **Model Development and Deployment:** building, training, validating, deploying ML models per industry/regulatory standards
- **Integration:** with data warehouse/lakehouse, BI tools, Simbanking, core banking, 3rd party systems
- **Governance and Compliance:** Tanzania Data Protection Act, Bank of Tanzania requirements, internal governance
- **Knowledge Transfer:** documentation, training, capability-building for internal teams
- **Support and Maintenance:** ongoing monitoring, model retraining, technical support
