## Scope of Work

### CRM System
- 360-degree centralized customer data view
- Contact management and customer segmentation
- Lead management with rule-based scoring
- Sales, marketing, and service automation
- Workflow automation and case management
- Customer satisfaction surveys
- Reports, dashboards, and BI integration with core banking
- Regulatory compliance (data protection, KYC)
- Integration with core banking, digital channels, and third-party platforms

### Call Centre
- Automatic Call Distribution (ACD), call routing, call hunting
- Interactive Voice Response (IVR) — English and Swahili
- Call recording (1-year retention, scalable to 200 calls/day)
- Call monitoring, analytics, management reporting (real-time and historical)
- Call transfer, 3-way calling, queue management, priority queue
- Wallboard dashboards, CTI, knowledge base
- Performance evaluation tools, workstation recording

### Channels
- Inbound: Telephone, Mobile, Email, SMS, Chat, Website
- Outbound: Telephone, Email, SMS, Mobile
- Web portal, social media (WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram), online chat, chatbots, VoIP
- Short code numbers, toll-free telephone lines

### Additional
- Chatbots for automated FAQs with escalation to agents
- Callback facility for requests and emergency calls
- Backup system to avoid transaction loss
- **3 years support and maintenance** from UAT/go-live
- **1 year Operation & Maintenance support** from go-live
