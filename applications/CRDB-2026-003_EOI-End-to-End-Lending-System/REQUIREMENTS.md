## Scope of Work

Design, implement, and integrate an End-to-End Lending Solution on top of **Temenos Transact R21** core banking platform and **WSO2** integration platform.

**Segments:** Consumer, MSME, and Corporate

**Full credit lifecycle:** origination, credit assessment, approval, disbursement, servicing, collections

**Key components:**
- Loan Origination and Workflow Layer (customer onboarding, application capture, document management)
- Credit Decisioning Layer (eligibility checks, scoring, policy rules, limits management, approval workflows)
- Integration with Core Banking (Temenos Transact R21) for account setup, disbursement, accruals, repayments, restructures
- Collateral Management
- Collections and Recovery
- Restructuring capabilities
- Enterprise Integration Layer (loosely coupled integrations)
- Data Warehouse, Reporting, and Analytics Layer

**Additional requirements:**
- Support segment-specific lending models including Agribusiness and Islamic financing
- Minimize customization within the CBS
- Agile, phased implementation approach
- Multi-entity support (Tanzania, Burundi, DRC)
