## Scope of Work

Implement a robust, customer-centric Trade Finance solution supporting:

- Online application of Trade Products with documentation
- LC and Guarantee draft generation
- Amendments and cancellations of LCs and Guarantees
- Electronic commercial document submission (e.g., Export LC)
- Real-time Trade Finance limits and exposure monitoring
- Digital review of commercial documents against LC terms
- Real-time tracking and status visibility (milestones, lifecycle updates)
- End-to-end workflow automation
- Reporting and Analytics
- User access and security
- Secure messaging and communication
- Data Privacy and Protection
- **Integration with Core Banking System**
- **Integration with 3rd party systems** (Supply Chain, Screening systems)
