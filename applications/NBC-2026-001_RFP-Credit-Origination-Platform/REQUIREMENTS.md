## Scope of Work

SaaS-based, configurable, secure, and scalable Commercial Credit Origination and Assessment Platform:

**A. Entity Management** — Companies, Bank FIs, Non-Bank FIs, Sovereigns, Individuals. Search, creation, duplicate prevention, watchlist tracking, officer assignments, industry coding, shareholding, credit/legal hierarchy management.

**B. Financial Analysis Module** — Chart of account grid for spreading historical financials and scenario-based projections; standard ratio calculations; templates for Corporate, General Banking, Agriculture, Insurance/Pension, Banking FIs; User-Defined Analysis for custom KPIs.

**C. PD Risk Grading Module** — Linked to Financial Analysis for quantitative scoring; qualitative augmentation; Grade/PD mapping; group borrower grading; sanctioner override capability.

**D. Credit Proposal Workflow** — Full lifecycle: Initiation → Entity updates → Financial Spreading → Risk Grading → Facility/Collateral → LGD → Covenants → Credit Memo → Multi-level Sanctioning → Appeals → Client Response → Publishing. SLA-based notifications.

**E. Deal Structuring** — Facilities, collateral/mitigants, LGD analysis, covenant management, credit memo generation, MOF or standalone product structuring, underlying asset management.

**F. Role-Based Access Control** — Edit vs. read-only by workflow stage, mandate validations, SSO (SAML/OIDC), RBAC, field-level controls, full audit trail.

**G. Integration** — Core Banking & CIF, Identity & Access (SSO), Document Management, Email/SMTP, Credit Data Providers, Reporting/Analytics (data warehouse/BI export).

**H. Alerts and Notifications** — Configurable at client, facility, financial spreading, and covenant levels.

**I. Training** — Comprehensive train-the-trainer approach.

**Implementation Timeline (Phased, within 2 years):**
- Phase 0: Mobilization (2–4 weeks)
- Phase 1: Core Build — Entity Mgmt, Financial Analysis, Core Workflow (8–12 weeks)
- Phase 2: Risk & Structuring — RG, Deal Structuring, LGD, Covenants, Credit Memo (8–10 weeks)
- Phase 3: Approvals & Publishing (4–6 weeks)
- Phase 4: Integrations & Migration (parallel)
- Phase 5: Testing, Training & Go-Live (4–6 weeks)
