## About

Isale Investment Limited is a multidisciplinary Tanzanian firm operating across engineering, industrial supply, ICT systems, and business consulting. Formerly **Leeds Company Limited**, the company changed its name in May 2023. We are duly licensed and registered to provide professional consultancy services, bound by a Code of Ethics that requires we only provide *"practical advice that works"*.

### Mission

To empower Africa through sustainable investment, modern engineering, and innovative digital systems that deliver measurable impact.

### Vision

To become Africa's most trusted integrator of technology, engineering, and business transformation.
